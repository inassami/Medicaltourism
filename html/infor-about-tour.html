1-Medical tourism in Helwan:

The suburb of Helwan is located thirty kilometers from the heart of Cairo, to the east of the Nile River, which is about four kilometers away from its bank, and it rises about forty meters above sea level.

Helwan is characterized by its dry atmosphere, with a humidity not exceeding 58%, in addition to several mineral and sulfur springs that are unparalleled in the world in terms of purity and therapeutic benefit. A sulfur center for natural medicine and the treatment of pain and rheumatic diseases was established there.

The history of Helwan water treatment dates back to 1899, then the bathrooms were renewed in 1955. The Helwan Sulfur Center for Rheumatism and Natural Medicine was established in an elegant Arab Islamic style. Beautiful gardens, making it the ideal place for patients and convalescents to stay.

The center includes a group of doctors specializing in rheumatic diseases and natural medicine. They perform a thorough medical examination on patients before proceeding with their treatment. After determining the appropriate treatment for each case, the patient is placed under full medical supervision throughout his treatment and stay in the center. A special file is prepared for each patient, which is kept in the medical archives of the center for reference when needed, to facilitate follow-up of patients in their visits to the center. 2-Bahariya Oasis:

Bahariya Oasis is located in Giza Governorate, located 365 km southwest of Giza. The region combines the elements of historical and archaeological tourism with wonderful elements for medical tourism and environmental recovery.

Among the most important archaeological monuments in Bahariya Oasis are the tombs of the 26th Dynasty, the cemetery of sacred birds, the remains of the Roman Triumphal Arch, the ruins of the Temple of Isis, and the ruins of a temple dating back to the era of Alexander the Great. , Where he discovered a huge number of golden mummies amounting to ten thousand. The author of this great discovery was the famous Egyptologist, Dr. Zahi Hawass.

In the tomb of the ruler of the oases (the 26th Pharaonic family), a hundred wonderful golden pieces were discovered, in addition to many golden statues of the gods.

There are about 400 wells in the Bahariya Oasis for warm and cold mineral and sulfur water, which researches conducted by Egyptian universities, national research centers and foreign scientific centers have proven their therapeutic value in rheumatoid and rheumatoid diseases and skin diseases, which qualifies them to become one of the most important therapeutic resorts in the world for its distinction of mild dry climate and bright sun throughout general.

he Bahariya Oasis is very popular with the tourists of central, western and northern Europe who go to it for hospitalization, especially in the area of Oyoun Bir Halfa, which has warm waters with a temperature of 45 degrees Celsius, and the area of Oyoun Al-Qasaa (30-40 degrees Celsius), and the areas of historical Roman wells and the well-known Bishmo well, which derives its water from Two sources, one cold and the other hot, end in one deep rocky stream.

Medical tourism facilities in Bahariya Oasis: Al Ain Sokhna Hotel has already been established at the highest level in Bahariya Oasis. The hotel includes 50 beds, 24 rooms, a gymnasium, a swimming pool and a sauna. He also laid the foundation stone for a specialized international center for environmental recovery to benefit from the therapeutic value of hundreds of wells and natural springs scattered in the region.

3-Siwa:

Siwa is located to the west of Marsa Matrouh, about 300 km. It occupies the forefront among the ideal places for medical tourism and natural recovery in Egypt. It is characterized by charming poetic calm, purity of the atmosphere, clear sky, and moderate temperature.

Dakrour Mountain It is located in the southeast of the Siwa Oasis, and the citizens of Siwa consider it a sacred mountain, and they hold an annual celebration on it in the month of October of every year after the harvest season (the valley season). It lasts three days and all the people participate in it. The mountain has acquired among the people since ancient times a therapeutic importance in rheumatic diseases, joint pain, and a general feeling of weakness and weakness. The treatment is based on sheikhs who specialize in burying the body in sand (burial therapy) for periods ranging between a quarter of an hour and half an hour a day for two weeks in the summer months, during specific hours of the day. The reputation of this type of environmental treatment became so popular that Mount Dakrour became a famous destination for medical tourism, frequented by Egyptians, Arab brothers, and foreigners alike.

4-The New Valley:

The New Valley Governorate is one of the largest governorates in Egypt, as it occupies about 37.6% of the total area of Egypt. The history of Egypt in all ages is embodied in the New Valley, where many monuments spread from the Pharaonic, Ptolemaic, Roman, Coptic and Islamic eras.

The New Valley has a special human character, as its residents are distinguished by unique customs and traditions, and they are also proficient in a number of industries and crafts that they have been famous for for a long time

The New Valley enjoys a wonderful climate throughout the year, characterized by dryness, devoid of moisture, bright sun, and an air that is impeccably pure from air pollutants in cities and crowded places.

The area is also spread with beautiful soft sands, herbs and medicinal plants from which medicines, vegetable and aromatic oils are extracted. The region is also rich in springs and natural wells, whose temperature rises to 34 degrees Celsius, and contains many useful mineral elements.

5-ELkharga:

Boulaq wells: It is located about 28 km south of the city of Al-Kharga. These are deep, self-flowing wells that spring at a depth of 1000 meters. Their temperature is 28 degrees Celsius, and they are surrounded by greenery. Laboratory analysis proved that its water contains several mineral elements of therapeutic benefit, and soft sand dunes are spread near it, which can be used for burial treatment in the sand (burial therapy) in joint diseases such as rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, and pain resulting from atrophy of the cartilage of the dorsal, lumbar, and sacral vertebrae. A rest house was established to serve the pioneers of the region, and a tourist resort is currently being built near it. The wells of Bulaq are not far from the main road that connects Kharga with the village of Paris, which leads to Luxor and the South Valley region. It is also close to tourist and archaeological sites.

Nasser Wells Group: It is located 18 kilometers south of Al-Kharga. It consists of three wells of different depths, the waters of which gather in a swimming pool with a water temperature of 28 degrees Celsius throughout the year. This pool is used to treat rheumatic diseases, chronic pain, skin diseases, kidney stones accompanied by renal colic, and digestive disorders. This group of wells is surrounded by a patch of green land with beautiful gardens surrounded by bright dunes with soft, pure sand. A camp has been set up on it, including accommodations and a cafeteria to serve the pioneers of the area.

6-Dakhla:

Mutt wells: It is located three kilometers from the city of Mut, the capital of Dakhla. It is a group of self-flowing wells, originating from a depth of 1224 meters. These wells are characterized by their hot water, which has a temperature of 43 degrees Celsius. According to laboratory analyzes conducted by the Health Affairs in the governorate, the water of these wells contains many mineral elements that are therapeutically useful in cases of rheumatism, psoriasis, and physical pain. Two swimming pools were built next to these wells, one for adults and the other for children. Bedrooms were also set up for the residents of the area. It was also provided with a restaurant and cafeteria. The area is connected to a paved road, and it is located on the road leading to the tourist attractions.

Ain al-Jabal well: It is located 52 kilometers from the city of Mut. This well has recently exploded, and no physical therapy facilities have been established in it yet. Its water temperature is 54 degrees Celsius.

7-Farafra:

This deep self-flowing well is located six kilometers west of the city of Farafra, and its water temperature reaches 24 degrees Celsius throughout the year. A tourist village was established near it, which includes a restaurant, cafeteria, swimming pool and green spaces. The elements of natural and environmental tourism meet in the area. And there is the world-famous White Desert, which is visited by tourists from all over the world. Farafra Oasis is linked to other oases and the Nile Valley by a good network of land transportation.

8-Sinai:

There are many hot water springs in Sinai, such as the Bath of Pharaoh and the Baths of Moses, which are of historical importance. These baths also have a therapeutic importance due to the fact that they contain sulfur water. In addition to all this, the moderate climate, the unique location on the shore of the Gulf of Suez, the sanctity of the region and its special location in the heart of the adherents of the three monotheistic religions. Pharaoh's bath: The Baths of Pharaoh are located on the Gulf of Suez, 250 km from Cairo, and they are a group of hot sulfur springs with a temperature of 27 degrees Celsius. Adjacent to sea water, and at the top there is a rocky cave carved into the mountain, which is used as a natural sauna due to the emission of heat from the hot sulfur water from the bottom of the cave to the top.

And by analyzing the water in terms of its chemical, bacteriological and natural properties, its excellent validity and effectiveness in treating many diseases has been proven, the most important of which are rheumatoid arthritis and rheumatism of all kinds, digestive system diseases, kidney diseases, lung allergies, liver diseases, skin diseases, and stadium injuries. The concentration of sulfur in this water is one of the highest levels of this element in mineral waters in the world. In addition to all this, the mild climate throughout the year, the dry weather, and the vast areas of warm sand that can be used in natural treatment, which are surrounded by a chain of mountains . These wonderful ingredients motivated the establishment of a tourist health resort under the name "Pharaoh's Baths Resort" (still under construction).
